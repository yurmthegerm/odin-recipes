Brie Pasta

Description

Brie pasta made with just four ingredients, freshly made pasta, brie cheese, roma tomatoes, and fresh basil is a simple pasta recipe perfect for summer.

Ingredients

  • pasta of your choice
  • 1 block of brie cheese
  • 2-3 roma tomatoes
  • fresh basil leaves

Steps

  1. Cook pasta of your choice in salted water until al dente. Save a cup of pasta water for later.
  2. Break brie cheese into pieces. Chop basil leaves and tomatoes.
  3. Combine all ingredients in a serving bowl large enough to hold the cooked pasta and the mixture.
  4. Stir gently to combine. If the mixture doesn't combine well, add pasta water.
